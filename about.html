About Us

Gembi Uka

CEO

Gembi is an accomplished electrical engineer, specializing in the field of electric power. He pursued his education at the esteemed Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Prishtina "Hasan Prishtina." Throughout his academic journey, Gembi developed a deep passion for green energy and its potential to transform the world. Motivated by his desire to create a better living environment for future generations, Gembi embarked on an entrepreneurial path. He founded his own solar panel company, aiming to contribute to a world powered by clean and sustainable energy. Drawing upon his extensive expertise and experience, Gembi dedicated himself to designing and implementing innovative solar panel solutions.
